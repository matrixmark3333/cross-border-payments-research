第5章导言Chapter 5 Introduction
为什么基础设施是跨境支付成本与速度的根本决定因素？Why Infrastructure Determines Cross-Border Payment Cost & Speed

当你通过银行向海外汇款时，这笔钱并不是像电子邮件一样"直接发过去"的。它要经过一条由多家银行组成的链条——每一个环节都可能扣费、等待、甚至拒绝。这条链条，就是代理行网络（Correspondent Banking Network），它是过去50年跨境支付的核心基础设施。When you send money abroad through a bank, the funds do not transfer "directly" like an email. They travel through a chain of multiple banks — each link capable of deducting fees, causing delays, or even rejecting the payment. This chain is the Correspondent Banking Network, the core infrastructure of cross-border payments for the past 50 years.

代理行模式的核心逻辑是：A国银行在B国没有直接运营资质，就必须委托一家在B国有账户的银行（代理行）来完成支付。一笔跨境支付通常要经过2到5家中间银行，每家都要检查合规、收取费用、等待结算窗口。这就是为什么跨境支付"慢而贵"的根本原因。The core logic of the correspondent banking model: a bank in country A has no direct operating license in country B, so it must commission a bank with accounts in country B (the correspondent) to complete the payment. A cross-border payment typically passes through 2-5 intermediary banks, each checking compliance, charging fees, and waiting for settlement windows — the fundamental reason cross-border payments are "slow and expensive."

G20于2020年制定的跨境支付改进路线图，明确提出到2027年实现：零售成本低于1%、速度90%在1小时内到达、更高透明度、更广覆盖范围。但2024年的监测数据显示，这四项目标均未达标。The G20 cross-border payment improvement roadmap, established in 2020, explicitly targets by 2027: retail cost below 1%, 90% of payments arriving within 1 hour, higher transparency, and broader coverage. However, 2024 monitoring data shows all four targets remain unmet.

本章从三个维度解析这一问题：第一，现有清算网络（SWIFT/CIPS）是如何运作的，改造进展如何；第二，即时跨境支付技术路径是什么；第三，稳定币和CBDC能否真正颠覆现有体系。This chapter analyzes the issue from three dimensions: first, how existing clearing networks (SWIFT/CIPS) work and the modernization progress; second, what the technology pathway for instant cross-border payments is; third, whether stablecoins and CBDCs can truly disrupt the existing system.

5.1 清算结算网络：SWIFT / CIPS / 代理行体系如何运作5.1 Clearing & Settlement Networks: How SWIFT / CIPS / Correspondent Banking Works
🔵 核心结论：Key Conclusion: 现有清算网络的技术现代化进展显著Existing clearing networks have made significant technical modernization progress（SWIFT gpi使90%支付在30分钟内到达），但成本居高不下的根本原因是代理行体系的结构性问题——预资金要求、合规成本分摊、运营时间错位——这些不是升级消息格式就能解决的。CIPS的快速增长（2024年金额+43%）代表另一条路：为特定货币建立主权清算渠道。

代理行网络为什么这么慢这么贵？想象一家尼日利亚中型银行想给一家中国供应商付款。这家尼日利亚银行在中国没有账户，所以它要委托一家美国的代理行（通常是摩根大通或花旗）；这家美国代理行再找它在中国的合作银行；最后由中国银行将钱汇入供应商账户。整条链上，每个节点都有：等待批处理时间（RTGS系统平均每周只运营66小时，有效结算窗口06:00-11:00 UTC）、AML/KYC检查时间、手续费扣除。一笔支付平均涉及5个接触点，其中1-3%会产生"例外与查询（E&I）"，需要人工介入解决。[card-071]Why is the correspondent banking network so slow and expensive? Imagine a mid-sized Nigerian bank wanting to pay a Chinese supplier. The Nigerian bank has no account in China, so it commissions a US correspondent (typically JPMorgan or Citi); the US correspondent then finds its Chinese partner bank; finally the Chinese bank wires funds to the supplier. Along the entire chain, every node has: batch processing wait time (RTGS systems average only 66 operating hours/week, effective settlement window 06:00-11:00 UTC), AML/KYC check time, and fee deductions. A payment averages 5 touchpoints, with 1-3% generating "exceptions and investigations (E&I)" requiring manual intervention. [card-071]

这就解释了为什么全球跨境支付成本居高不下——代理行每个节点都要覆盖合规成本、流动性成本和运营成本，而这些成本最终都分摊给了汇款人。This explains why global cross-border payment costs remain high — every correspondent banking node must cover compliance, liquidity, and operational costs, all of which are ultimately passed on to the remitter.

SWIFT的核心地位及gpi升级效果

SWIFT（环球银行间金融电信协会）成立于1973年，目前连接200+国的11,000+家金融机构，处理全球绝大多数批发跨境支付的报文传输（注意：SWIFT传的是"支付指令"，不是钱本身）。

2017年推出的SWIFT gpi（全球支付创新）是SWIFT历史上最重要的升级。核心变化是引入UETR（唯一端到端交易参考号），让支付像快递包裹一样可以全程追踪；同时要求参与银行当天（同日）完成支付处理，不能隔日积压。效果显著：到2019年底，60%的SWIFT国际转账已使用gpi；到2024年，gpi支付的90%在30分钟内完成机构间结算，已超越G20的2027年速度目标（90%在1小时内）。[card-151]

但gpi改善的是机构间的速度——终端用户感受到的速度还受到最后一公里（受款行内部处理）的影响。FSB数据显示，2024年批发跨境支付仅有50.6%能在1小时内到达终端受益人，零售支付仅有33.5%——这与gpi机构间的90%存在巨大落差。[card-066]

此外，gpi还规划了三项进阶升级：DLT用于nostro账户实时对账、止付/召回服务、以及gpi COVER（中间行透明度）。这些功能进一步压缩代理行体系的效率损耗。[card-151]

ISO 20022迁移：行业最大一次技术升级ISO 20022 Migration: The Industry's Largest Technology Upgrade

ISO 20022是新一代支付消息标准，相比旧的MT格式，它能携带更丰富的结构化数据（完整的收款人信息、汇款原因、发票号码等）。这听起来是小事，但影响是系统性的：ISO 20022 is the next-generation payment messaging standard; compared to the old MT format, it carries richer structured data (complete beneficiary information, remittance reason, invoice numbers, etc.). This sounds minor but the impact is systemic:

① 大幅减少人工干预：传统1-3%的支付产生"例外与查询"案例，需要人工核实，ISO 20022让这类案例的自动化处理率达84%，每年为行业节省3.32亿美元运营成本+2.7亿美元流动性成本。[card-071] ② 降低AML误报：目前5-10%的支付触发合规警报，其中99%是误报（浪费大量合规资源），ISO 20022可将误报率降低25-30%。[card-071] ③ 数据互操作：统一格式使SWIFT、本地即时支付系统、CBDC平台之间的数据交换更顺畅。[card-152]

截至2024年底，全球ISO 20022采用率仅32.9%，日均已有140万条CBPR+（跨境支付ISO 20022）报文。2025年11月是SWIFT全面强制切换截止日，届时MT格式将在跨境支付中被淘汰。这是SWIFT 40年历史上最大的单次技术升级，约140+个司法管辖区正在积极推进。[card-152, card-066]As of end-2024, global ISO 20022 adoption rate is only 32.9%, with 1.4M CBPR+ (cross-border ISO 20022) messages daily. November 2025 is the SWIFT mandatory full switch deadline, when MT format will be retired from cross-border payments. This is the largest single technical upgrade in SWIFT's 40-year history, with 140+ jurisdictions actively advancing. [card-152, card-066]

对未能在2025年11月完成迁移的机构，直接后果是无法通过SWIFT发送或接收跨境支付——这是无可妥协的刚性合规要求，正驱动行业加速行动。[card-152]For institutions unable to complete migration by November 2025, the direct consequence is inability to send or receive cross-border payments via SWIFT — this is a non-negotiable hard compliance requirement driving industry acceleration. [card-152]

清算网络现代化的主要障碍

尽管技术层面的升级（gpi、ISO 20022）已经取得显著成效，但清算网络的根本性成本障碍依然存在：

🔍 主要结构性障碍（非技术问题）Key Structural Barriers (Non-Technical)
  • 预资金（Prefunding）锁定：代理行在各市场需要维持大量nostro账户余额，这些沉淀资金的机会成本最终转化为手续费。Prefunding Lock-up: Correspondent banks must maintain large nostro account balances in each market; the opportunity cost of these idle funds is ultimately converted into fees.
  • AML/CFT碎片化：不同司法区的反洗钱规则不一致，导致每笔支付需要在多个节点重复筛查，不统一的制裁名单执行使成本上升、速度降低。[card-066]AML/CFT Fragmentation: Inconsistent anti-money laundering rules across jurisdictions require each payment to be screened repeatedly at multiple nodes; inconsistent sanctions list enforcement raises costs and slows speed. [card-066]
  • 代理行关系收缩：近年来全球代理行数量持续减少（合规成本上升导致银行选择退出服务不足的市场），偏远走廊的支付选择越来越少，成本越来越高。Correspondent Banking Contraction: The number of global correspondent banking relationships has been declining (rising compliance costs drive banks to exit underserved markets); remote corridors have fewer payment options and higher costs.
  • 运营时间错位：RTGS系统平均每周仅运营66小时（理论最大168小时），全球"结算窗口"仅为06:00-11:00 UTC的5小时重叠区。[card-071]Operating Hours Mismatch: RTGS systems average only 66 operating hours per week (theoretical max 168 hours); the global "settlement window" is only a 5-hour overlap from 06:00-11:00 UTC. [card-071]
  • 数据本地化要求：各国不同的数据主权规定迫使支付处理者在每个司法区维护单独的数据处理流程，显著增加运营成本。[card-066]Data Localization Requirements: Different national data sovereignty rules force payment processors to maintain separate data processing workflows in each jurisdiction, significantly increasing operational costs. [card-066]

5.2 即时跨境支付：FPS互联、Project Nexus与Fintech路径5.2 Instant Cross-Border Payments: FPS Linkage, Project Nexus & Fintech
🟢 核心结论：Key Conclusion: 即时跨境支付Instant cross-border payment is 技术上已经可行technically feasible，亚洲多个走廊已实现实时互联（PayNow-PromptPay、UPI-PayNow）。Project Nexus 2024年完成蓝图设计，目标是通过"单次接入"连接17亿人的FPS网络，预计2026-2027年进入商业运营。Fintech（Nium、Airwallex等）通过自建流动性网络+银行账户体系，在部分走廊已实现比银行更快的到账速度。主要障碍不是技术，而是治理协调和监管互认。

什么是即时跨境支付？What is Instant Cross-Border Payment?

国内即时支付系统（Instant Payment Systems，IPS / FPS）早已成熟——印度UPI每天处理4亿+笔交易，泰国PromptPay、新加坡PayNow也已是日常支付工具。问题在于：这些系统各自封闭，互不相通。将它们"接起来"实现跨境即时汇款，就是过去五年最重要的支付基础设施课题。Domestic instant payment systems (IPS/FPS) are already mature — India's UPI processes 400M+ transactions daily; Thailand's PromptPay and Singapore's PayNow are everyday tools. The challenge: these systems are each closed and not interconnected. "Linking them up" for instant cross-border remittances has been the most important payment infrastructure challenge of the past five years.

双边互联（两个系统之间建立连接）已经有多个活跃案例。但双边模式有根本缺陷：N个国家要实现完全互联，需要建立N×(N-1)/2条双边链路——20个国家就需要190条。这在治理和维护成本上都不可持续。Bilateral linkage (establishing connections between two systems) already has multiple live cases. But the bilateral model has a fundamental flaw: N countries achieving full interconnection requires N×(N-1)/2 bilateral links — 20 countries alone requires 190. This is unsustainable in governance and maintenance costs.

Fintech的即时到账路径：绕开传统轨道的智慧Fintech's Instant Payment Path: The Art of Bypassing Traditional Rails

Wise、Airwallex、Nium等Fintech公司实现"即时到账"的方式，并非通过连接各国央行系统。它们的核心策略是：在各主要市场预先持有本地货币余额（或账户），当用户在A国发起支付时，公司内部在A国扣款，同时从B国的账户立刻付款给收款人。整个过程绕开了代理行链条，用内部流动性管理取代了外部结算延迟。Wise, Airwallex, Nium and other Fintech companies achieve "instant delivery" not by connecting to each country's central bank systems. Their core strategy is: pre-holding local currency balances (or accounts) in each major market. When a user in country A initiates a payment, the company internally debits in country A while simultaneously crediting from its country B account to the recipient. The entire process bypasses the correspondent chain, replacing external settlement delays with internal liquidity management.

以Nium为例：2024年10月宣布金融机构可通过现有SWIFT基础设施直接接入其全球实时支付网络，80%以上的支付在15分钟内完成。[card-086] 这个模式同时兼容MT和ISO 20022格式，大幅降低了金融机构的对接成本，也为自己在MT向ISO 20022迁移过渡期创造了粘性中间件地位。

但这种模式有其边界：它在流量大、走廊稳定的主要市场效果极佳，但在小众走廊（如西非→东欧）很难维持足够的本地流动性，最终仍可能回到代理行轨道。But this model has its limits: it works extremely well in high-volume, stable corridors for major markets, but small-niche corridors (e.g., West Africa → Eastern Europe) struggle to maintain sufficient local liquidity, ultimately still relying on correspondent banking.

非银行PSP接入FPS：竞争格局的政策杠杆Non-Bank PSP Access to FPS: Policy Leverage for Competition

目前全球约1/3的FPS允许非银行PSP直接接入（无需通过银行中间层）。预计到2025年底，这一比例将达到50%，其中欧洲和中东非洲贡献了全部计划扩展。[card-071] 这对Stripe、Airwallex等Fintech公司意义重大——直接接入意味着可以自主掌握结算速度和成本，而不必受制于合作银行的处理时间。Currently ~1/3 of global FPS allow non-bank PSPs direct access (without going through bank intermediaries). By end-2025, this share is expected to reach 50%, with Europe and MENA contributing all planned expansions. [card-071] This is significant for Fintech companies like Stripe and Airwallex — direct access means controlling settlement speed and costs rather than being constrained by partner bank processing times.

另一个值得关注的数据：63%的FPS/RTGS系统允许有本地实体的外资银行直接接入，但无本地存在的外资银行仅有14%的系统能接入。[card-071] 这意味着在没有本地执照的市场，跨境Fintech仍然依赖代理行——本地化合规成本依然是行业护城河。Another data point worth noting: 63% of FPS/RTGS systems allow direct access for foreign banks with local entities, but only 14% of systems allow access for foreign banks without local presence. [card-071] This means that in markets without local licenses, cross-border Fintech still relies on correspondents — local compliance costs remain an industry moat.

理解这三种"新型支付货币"的本质区别Understanding the Essential Differences Between These Three "New Payment Currencies"

很多人将稳定币、零售CBDC、批发CBDC混为一谈，但它们解决的问题、服务的对象、面临的障碍完全不同。简单类比：

— 批发CBDC：央行发行给银行用的"超级支票"，用于机构间大额实时结算，取代现有RTGS和SWIFT。— Wholesale CBDC: "Super-checks" issued by central banks to banks, for large-value real-time settlement between institutions, replacing existing RTGS and SWIFT.

— 零售CBDC：央行发行给普通人用的"数字现金"，相当于电子版的人民币/美元钞票。— Retail CBDC: "Digital cash" issued by central banks to ordinary people, equivalent to digital versions of RMB/USD banknotes.

— 稳定币：私人机构（Circle、Tether等）发行的、与法币1:1挂钩的加密代币，可以通过区块链实现7×24小时全球流通，无需央行参与。— Stablecoin: Crypto tokens issued by private entities (Circle, Tether, etc.) pegged 1:1 to fiat currencies, enabling 24/7 global circulation via blockchain without central bank involvement.

mBridge：批发CBDC最先进的多边平台mBridge: The Most Advanced Multilateral Platform for Wholesale CBDC

Project mBridge于2024年6月达到MVP（最小可行产品）阶段——这是全球批发型跨境CBDC最重要的里程碑。它不是单个央行的实验，而是5个主权央行共建的多边结算平台：中国人民银行（数字货币研究所）、香港金融管理局、泰国央行、阿联酋央行，加上2024年6月新加入的沙特中央银行。[card-135]Project mBridge reached MVP (Minimum Viable Product) stage in June 2024 — the most important milestone for wholesale cross-border CBDC globally. It is not a single central bank experiment but a multilateral settlement platform co-built by 5 sovereign central banks: PBOC (Digital Currency Institute), HKMA, Bank of Thailand, CBUAE, plus Saudi Central Bank joining in June 2024. [card-135]

mBridge的核心价值主张简单而有力：让机构间的大额跨境支付实现7×24小时实时结算，无需代理行中介。技术架构上，每家参与央行在本国部署一个验证节点，没有单点控制，通过共识机制完成结算——这种去中心化设计是回应"谁控制平台"政治敏感性的技术答案。[card-135]mBridge's core value proposition is simple and powerful: enabling 7×24 real-time settlement for large-value cross-border payments between institutions, without correspondent banking intermediaries. Each participating central bank deploys a validation node in its home country, with no single point of control, settling through consensus mechanisms — this decentralized design is the technical answer to the politically sensitive question of "who controls the platform." [card-135]

2024年10月，中国银行（香港）成为首家接入mBridge的商业银行，标志着平台从央行实验向商业应用的实质性跨越。[card-135] BIS创新中心将逐步移交技术顾问角色，由各央行组建的NSO（Nexus方案组织）接管独立运营。In October 2024, Bank of China (Hong Kong) became the first commercial bank to connect to mBridge, marking a substantive transition from central bank experiment to commercial application. [card-135] BIS Innovation Hub will gradually transfer the technical advisor role to the NSO (Nexus Scheme Organisation) formed by the central banks for independent operation.

稳定币的规模化现实：$27.6万亿已超越Visa+Mastercard合计Stablecoin Scale Reality: $27.6T Surpasses Visa+Mastercard Combined

这个数字很容易让人困惑。稳定币结算量超过Visa+Mastercard，是真的吗？This number is easily confusing. Is it true that stablecoin settlement volume exceeds Visa+Mastercard combined?

答案是：基本是真的，但需要理解其结构。USDC 2024年全年链上结算量达$27.6万亿，这个数字包含了大量金融机构间的批发转账（而非零售消费）。稳定币的主要使用场景不是"你去超市刷卡"，而是金融机构之间的流动性管理和清算。[card-125] 但这恰恰是传统跨境支付成本最高、效率最低的环节。The answer: essentially yes, but the structure must be understood. USDC 2024 annual on-chain settlement reached $27.6T — this includes substantial wholesale transfers between financial institutions (not retail spending). Stablecoins' primary use case is not "swiping at a supermarket" but liquidity management and clearing between financial institutions. [card-125] But this is precisely the highest-cost, lowest-efficiency part of traditional cross-border payments.

Thunes与Circle的合作提供了最具说服力的企业级案例：传统模式下，Thunes在各新兴市场维持大量nostro账户预资金（3-5个工作日结算，大量沉淀资金）；接入USDC后，加纳T+2→同日，菲律宾T+1→T+0，南非半天→数分钟。USDC资金量每月增长约1/3，9个月内接入双位数合作伙伴。[card-091]The Thunes-Circle collaboration provides the most compelling enterprise case: in the traditional model, Thunes maintains large nostro account prefunding in emerging markets (3-5 business day settlement, massive idle funds); after adopting USDC, Ghana T+2→same day, Philippines T+1→T+0, South Africa half-day→minutes. USDC funding volume grows ~1/3 monthly, with double-digit partners onboarded in 9 months. [card-091]

这个案例揭示了稳定币对跨境支付基础设施的真正价值：不是"去掉银行"，而是解放被锁死在nosro账户里的流动性——让资金动起来，而不是躺在账户里等待结算窗口。This case reveals stablecoins' true value for cross-border payment infrastructure: not "eliminating banks," but liberating liquidity locked up in nostro accounts — making funds move, rather than sitting in accounts waiting for settlement windows.

给行业参与者的战略启示

对于商业银行：ISO 20022迁移是2025年最紧迫的合规事项，同时需要评估是否在mBridge和Project Nexus生态中占据早期节点位置。For commercial banks: ISO 20022 migration is the most urgent compliance task for 2025, while also needing to assess whether to occupy early node positions in the mBridge and Project Nexus ecosystems.

对于跨境支付Fintech：SWIFT gpi+本地FPS接入的"双轨策略"是当前最优路径；同时密切跟踪稳定币监管进展，尤其是美国GENIUS Act和欧盟MiCA的实施细则——这直接影响稳定币作为结算层的合法性时间窗口。For cross-border payment Fintech: the "dual-rail strategy" of SWIFT gpi + local FPS access is the current optimal path; also closely track stablecoin regulatory progress, especially US GENIUS Act and EU MiCA implementation details — these directly affect the legitimacy time window for stablecoins as a settlement layer.

对于企业（B端用户）：在主流走廊（美国-东南亚、欧洲-亚洲），选择已接入多种实时轨道（SWIFT gpi + 本地FPS + USDC备选）的供应商，可以获得最高的速度和成本优化。新兴市场走廊（非洲、南亚）短期内代理行仍是主要通道，但稳定币渗透速度正在加快。For enterprises (B-side users): in mainstream corridors (US-Southeast Asia, Europe-Asia), choosing suppliers connected to multiple real-time rails (SWIFT gpi + local FPS + USDC as backup) provides maximum speed and cost optimization. In emerging market corridors (Africa, South Asia), correspondent banking remains the primary channel short-term, but stablecoin penetration is accelerating.
