🔄 重复KYC浪费Repeated KYC Waste
每个代理行独立进行KYC/AML核查，同一笔支付被检查3–5次。ISO 20022迁移后可将误报率降低25–30%（BIS #071）Each correspondent bank performs independent KYC/AML checks; the same payment is verified 3–5 times. ISO 20022 migration can reduce false positive rate by 25–30% (BIS #071)
⏰ RTGS时间窗口RTGS Time Window
全球RTGS平均运营66小时/周，仅39%覆盖率。夜间/周末支付进入队列等待，是T+1/T+2的主因（BIS #071）Global RTGS average 66hrs/week, only 39% coverage. Overnight/weekend payments queue up — the main cause of T+1/T+2 delays (BIS #071)
📨 串行报文传递Sequential Message Relay
SWIFT MT格式串行处理，每笔支付经历多个中间节点顺序转发。gpi引入UETR后90%可在30分钟内到达（card-151）SWIFT MT format processed sequentially — each payment passes through multiple intermediary nodes in sequence. With gpi UETR, 90% can reach within 30 minutes (card-151)
💸 隐性外汇差价Hidden FX Spread
外汇差价约占总成本30–50%，且不透明。全球仅55.6%的PSP向用户提供速度和成本信息（FSB #066）FX spread accounts for ~30–50% of total costs and is opaque. Only 55.6% of PSPs globally provide users with speed and cost information (FSB #066)
