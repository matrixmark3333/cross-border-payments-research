📊 横向对比四大核心发现4 Core Findings from Cross-Company Comparison
  • 规模两极分化：Revolut交易量$1.3万亿 vs OFX $38B，差距35倍。但规模≠盈利，OFX利润率（28.4%）与Wise底层EBITDA（28.4%）完全一致，说明"大客户高接触"模式能追平"规模低费率"模式的盈利水平。Scale Polarization: Revolut TPV $1.3T vs. OFX $38B—35× gap. But scale ≠ profitability: OFX's margin (28.4%) matches Wise's underlying EBITDA (28.4%) exactly, demonstrating that a "high-touch large-client" model can equal the profitability of a "scale low-fee" model.
  • Take Rate揭示客群本质：Remitly 2.32%（移民零售汇款）vs Wise 0.58%（大额+企业+平台混合）vs dLocal 1.2%（新兴市场复杂性溢价）。费率不是高低优劣，是客群定位的映射。Take Rate Reveals Customer Base Essence: Remitly 2.32% (immigrant retail remittances) vs. Wise 0.58% (large amounts + enterprise + platform mix) vs. dLocal 1.2% (emerging market complexity premium). Fee rates are not good or bad—they map to customer positioning.
  • 增速领跑者皆为B端或混合型：Airwallex（+90%）、Revolut Business（+56%）、Payoneer B2B（+42%）都是企业业务驱动增长，纯C端汇款（WU核心业务）已趋向零增长。Growth Leaders Are All B2B or Hybrid: Airwallex (+90%), Revolut Business (+56%), Payoneer B2B (+42%) are all enterprise-driven. Pure consumer remittances (WU core business) are trending toward zero growth.
  • 利息收入的战略意义分化：Wise利息收入£594M占底层收入11%、Payoneer利息$257M占总营收26%，两者均依赖高利率环境红利；Remitly仅$8M利息说明"流转型"商业模式不积累余额。Strategic Significance of Interest Income Diverges: Wise interest income £594M represents 11% of underlying revenue; Payoneer interest $257M represents 26% of total revenue—both depend on high-rate environment tailwinds. Remitly's mere $8M interest reveals that a "flow-through" business model doesn't accumulate balances.

商业模式摘要Business Model Summary
  • 三层收入结构：跨境转账费（£840M，+6%）+ 卡片交换费（£220M，+31%）+ 其他/账户功能费（£152M，+71%）。跨境收入增速仅6%（因取费率连续下降），但非跨境收入高速增长正在补偿收入总量，这是"以量换价"战略的关键支撑。[card-001]Three-Tier Revenue Structure: Cross-border transfer fees (£840M, +6%) + Card interchange fees (£220M, +31%) + Other/account feature fees (£152M, +71%). Cross-border revenue grows at only 6% (due to continuous Take Rate declines), but non-cross-border revenue growth is offsetting total revenue—the critical support for a "trade price for volume" strategy. [card-001]
  • 利息收入是当前利润的隐形引擎：FY2025客户余额利息£594M，但超过1%收益率的"超额利息"（£444M）带来巨额报告利润（税前34%利润率 vs 底层21%）。这取决于高利率环境，一旦降息将大幅收窄。利率1%下行 = -£86M净利息收入。[card-001]Interest Income Is the Hidden Engine of Current Profits: FY2025 customer balance interest £594M, but "excess interest" above 1% yield (£444M) generates outsized reported profits (34% pre-tax margin vs. 21% underlying). This depends on the high-rate environment—a rate cut will significantly narrow it. 1% rate decline = -£86M net interest income. [card-001]
  • Platform战略突破主流：FY2025新增摩根士丹利、渣打银行、Nubank、Itaú Unibanco为合作伙伴。银行选择Wise Platform的价值主张：大幅削减跨境基础设施资本支出、几个月内即可集成（vs 自建需数年）。这是从B2C到B2B2X的战略升级，也是Wise未来增长的第二飞轮。[card-001]Platform Strategy Breaks into the Mainstream: FY2025 added Morgan Stanley, Standard Chartered, Nubank, and Itaú Unibanco as partners. Banks' value proposition for choosing Wise Platform: dramatically cut cross-border infrastructure capex, integrate within months (vs. years to build in-house). This is a strategic upgrade from B2C to B2B2X, and Wise's second growth flywheel. [card-001]
  • 营销费用极低是护城河之一：FY2025营销费仅£53.8M（占收入4.4%），而Remitly营销占收入24%。70%+新客来自口碑推荐，这是Wise最难复制的软护城河——产品体验驱动的口碑飞轮。相比Remitly7倍的营销费率，Wise每获取一个客户的边际成本极低。[card-001] [card-002]Minimal Marketing Cost Is One of the Moats: FY2025 marketing spend was only £53.8M (4.4% of revenue), while Remitly's marketing takes 24% of revenue. 70%+ of new customers come from word-of-mouth—Wise's hardest-to-replicate soft moat: a product-experience-driven referral flywheel. Compared to Remitly's 7× marketing ratio, Wise's marginal cost per new customer acquisition is extremely low. [card-001] [card-002]
  • 菲律宾直连案例量化了基础设施价值：FY2025接入菲律宾InstaPay，90%转账立即到达，成本降低8倍。计划FY2026再接入巴西PIX和日本Zengin，这是全球直连战略持续降本的可量化体现。[card-001]Philippines Direct Connection Quantifies Infrastructure Value: FY2025 connected to Philippines InstaPay: 90% of transfers arrive instantly, costs reduced 8×.   • 中期"三倍投入"承诺：新CFO（来自Delivery Hero）明确表态将三倍营销投入，同时加大技术和人才投入。当前利润率21%远超中期目标13–16%，意味着将把超额利润主动再投入到增长，维持竞争壁垒而非提前"躺平"收割。[card-001]Medium-Term "Triple Investment" Commitment: New CFO (from Delivery Hero) has clearly committed to tripling marketing spend while increasing tech and talent investment. 风险与挑战Risks & Challenges
  • 利率风险是最大的盈利隐患：当前高利率环境贡献了巨额报告利润，但底层利润（21%）才是可持续标准。若全球降息，利息收入将显著缩水，但Wise的定价逻辑（降低Take Rate以抢量）需要持续利润支撑投资——这个平衡点是最大的不确定性。[card-001]Interest Rate Risk Is the Biggest Profitability Threat: The current high-rate environment contributes outsized reported profits, but underlying profit (21%) is the sustainable baseline. If global rates fall, interest income will shrink significantly—yet Wise's pricing logic (reducing Take Rate to capture volume) requires sustained profit to fund investment. This balance point is the biggest uncertainty. [card-001]
  • CFPB同意令与监管拨备激增：FY2025法律监管拨备从£2.2M跳升至£13.6M（+519%），与美国CFPB同意令相关。全球监管收紧（美国、英国Consumer Duty、欧盟等）将持续抬高合规成本。[card-001]CFPB Consent Order and Regulatory Provisions Surge: FY2025 legal/regulatory provisions jumped from £2.2M to £13.6M (+519%), related to the US CFPB consent order.   • 企业客户增速放缓需密切关注：企业客户FY2025增速仅11%（vs 个人22%），部分因H2 FY2024暂停开户。虽Q4已恢复，但企业客户的重要性在于更高ARPU和Account余额贡献，若增速持续低迷将拖累整体客户质量提升的速度。[card-001]Slowing Business Customer Growth Warrants Close Monitoring: Business customers grew only 11% in FY2025 (vs. personal 22%), partly due to the H2 FY2024 account opening pause. [card-001]

  • 规模不等于护城河：Western Union $4.2B收入远超Wise $1.5B，但WU零增长而Wise +15%。规模在存量竞争中是优势，在增量市场中反而因组织惰性成为劣势。Scale Does Not Equal Moat: Western Union's $4.2B revenue far exceeds Wise's $1.5B, but WU has zero growth while Wise is +15%.
